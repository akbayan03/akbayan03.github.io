About us

Bringing the best of Sri Lanka to you...

We are a premier destination management company dedicated toward providing our customers with the best holiday experience in Sri Lanka. Our team of experts will plan and organize your trips and excursions to suit your preferences and your budget. From scenic train rides to jet skiing in the blue ocean, Sri Lanka has destinations and activities to suit anyone and everyone. We focus on offering a variety of specialized itineraries and an enticing range of activities in your travel plans.

Customers satisfaction is a main priority At FIDATO Travel and we provide much attention to detail to make your experience with us a momentous one. With the perfect travel itinerary curated just for you we will ensure that your holiday is hassle free and require minimum effort from your end. You just need to sit back and relax whilst we ensure everything goes smoothly for you. We strive to develop a long term relationship with you based on trust which is one of the core values at Fidato.

FIDATO Travel is your trusted travel partner to make your dream vacation a reality. Our personalised service coupled with unbeatable value for money, will assure the satisfaction of our clients at all times

We can help you plan out your trip to any of the amazing destinations the island of Sri Lanka has to offer. Get in touch with us for a holiday with memories to last a lifetime!
